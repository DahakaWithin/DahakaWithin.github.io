Progress and beyond, summer

The sun is shining, time to relax and hit the beach.. or maybe not. Time to get serious if we are to complete our goal of releasing the game in September. Plenty to do, check it out below.
First of all, I'd like to say we made a ton of progress thanks to all of our teams, design, hardware, game mechanics, everything and everyone pitched in and took their part to bring us just a little closer to open Alpha. You would think we would all get some rest as summer just started, you'd be wrong! Our focus is 100% on the game and experience of players in September, we said we'll do it, we will.
Our plan is to bring out a couple of smaller patches to polish some things we left in the dark, after that push out big updates so the game is ready for open testing later on. We want to be sure the game is functional and as bug free as we can get it for the release. If it won't be, we'll delay it a short while, with no trouble in sight right now, we should be on schedule.

Sit back, relax, grab yourself a beer, or a soda, it's our time to get the job done. Thanks for reading and see you all in the next notes.
Early Access is finally here! We're extremely excited to bring the build into playable state for our loyal fans that stuck with us for all this time, thank you for all your support and patience, Alpha is finally here!
