Patch notes 0.38, May

Here are the latest patch notes, graphic glitches fixed and more.

- Wall textures, after a long search, we finally found the glitch causing the water textures on the walls to change colours under certain angle, even though the colour display was impressive, it was a bit strange to find the ruined world in pink, no more pink elephants.

- Floor textures, water on these produced a very blurry effect, unrealistic and difficult to look at, replaced the water effect with a more stable one.

- Sky blocks, in short, cloud effects, textures and sun shining was all a bit dull, remade effects, improved realism, a brighter future for Metro.

Thanks for reading, see you all in the next notes.
