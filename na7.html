Early access release August/September

Early Access is finally here! First official announcement of early release has arrived.
After a long time, we can safely announce the release date for our game. Over the months we accomplished a lot and it's time for you to experience it, first hand. Consider this a sneak peek into our plans for the summer and beyond. After a long discussion with the head developers we agreed it's fair to announce the final release date for early access, open Alpha. Open Alpha means playing won't be limited to our supporters but available to all players who wish to participate and experience the game, this decision was made not to encourage competition over the keys and leave out a good portion of people out of it.

More information will be released in part 2 of out Road to Alpha, sneak peek, later on.

Thanks for reading, see you all in the next notes.
