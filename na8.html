Road to Alpha, part 1

Welcome to our first part of Road to Alpha, Road to Alpha is a way for players to get a sneak peek into our plans for releasing the game into open Alpha. Today we have a special guest, head developer of design team, Chris. Chris will be telling us a bit more about the plan, design and future of Metro 2035.

Hello there, I'm in charge of design team working on Metro 2035, our main focus over the last few mini patches has been to update and optimise the game for introduction of DirectX 12 and support for it. Jumping from DirectX 11 to 12 is a big leap, especially considering the hardware requirements and base engine compatibility, to skip the boring stuff, we're still adapting our systems to it, but making good progress, DirectX 12 will make a huge difference when it comes to visuals, texture quality and general appeal. These features are crucial for our plans for Alpha, when we finish our job, we'll sync in with other teams and get it all going. There's no set date when the release will be, not yet, however, with every patch, we're closer to announcing the real deal, open Alpha.

Thanks for reading, see you all in the next notes.
