Patch notes 0.37, April

Smoking guns.
Updated weapon models, bullet models, changed stats for each weapon.

In this patch we look at weapons, how they shoot, why they shoot that way and their appearance.

- Appearance, updated weapon models, rustier look, in line with the world around them, no more shiny perfections in the world of destruction, updated bullet models and visible bullets in ammo slots. Updated HUD visuals with a new font and better readability.

- Shooting mechanics, after messing around with firepower a bit, we noticed a strange feature of aiming down sights, visual crosshair did not match the actual shooting, even though the difference was really tiny we decided to fix the margin. In return, accurate shots are not magically missing, headshots on NPCs greatly improved, accurate shots rewarded as they should be.

- Balanced weapon power considering their ammo, maximum ammo and used bullets, sniper rifles maximum ammo decreased but power increased, revolver ammo increased, reload time increased.

Thanks for reading, see you all in the next notes.
