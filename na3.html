Patch notes 0.4, July

Better performance, introducing new lighting and changes on system requirements..

Changing system requirements was always a touchy subject, before you close the notes and flip your PC through the window bare with us and you might be surprised. Introducing new light effects and such into the game created a heavy load on the structure, demanding more on your hardware, even though we agreed no drastic changes were to be made to the game requirements we decided to keep the lighting as it was providing too good of an experience to be left out.
Now, that's not the whole story, Pete, our head hardware chief decided he wouldn't let this slide, we agreed with him, for the past weeks he's been hard at work to make sure that your hardware wouldn't need upgrading with the new lights, he did it!
After many tries Pete found an optimal way to keep the new features but reduce the stress on critical hardware, CPU and GPU of course.

What does that mean for you in the end? No major changes to the system requirements!
Because Pete made sure everything is optimised, you won't need to get the latest hardware, not even close, maybe a stick of RAM, but RAM never broke anyone's budget, right?

- Optimised frames across the game, some areas proved to be a bit stiff for some AMD users, fixed that (that Pete again), hopefully no more sticky frames areas, new lights are OP.

Let's wrap this up, thanks for reading and see you all in the next patch notes.
