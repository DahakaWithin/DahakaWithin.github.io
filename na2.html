Patch notes 0.5, August



Bugfixes, bugfixes and more bugs fixed...
Yep, it's that time again, more insects to be eradicated, pun aside, we're friendly towards our cyber bugs but they need to go. Here's a list of fixes below and context.

- Fixed lighting bugging out and some levels, pesky wall coding was causing a weird interaction with the lights, instead of picking out the walls out we fixed lights themselves, pretty neat, ha?

- Corrected the texture loading on floors and partial reflection on water, the layers themselves got a bit tangled up but we made sure this would not bother us anymore, we also made sure this would not affect walking, movement or interaction with items on the floor, yay for us!

- Polished item reload animations! Yes, yes, clunky animation on FS92 is no more, rearranged animation frames and made sure this would not happen again, silky smooth now, big thanks to Mike and the feedback on this bug.

- Collision on Bridge area is no more. Remember that Bridge section you could get stuck on if you tried really, really hard? No? Good, because you won't be getting stuck there anymore in the first place. Smaller terrain correction and such, nothing too special.

- Monster AI, now this one was tricky, on certain levels of the game pesky Watchers would lose their focus and decided to chill around the toxic terrain, taking a not so deserved vacation, although who are we to judge them, they did survive a nuclear disaster, sort of. Vacation's over boys, fixed AI tracking, made sure they weren't using ESP though, balanced aggro on players and gunshots.

That's it for this patch, thanks for the support and see you all in the next patch notes.
