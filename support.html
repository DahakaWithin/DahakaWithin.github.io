Kickstarter

What is Kickstarter?

Kickstarter is a new way to fund creative projects, from movies, games, music, art, design and much more. Kickstarter is full of projects, big and small, that are brought to life through the direct support of people like you. Since our launch in 2009, 9.3 million people have pledged more than $1.9 billion, funding 91,000 creative projects. Thousands of creative projects are raising funds on Kickstarter right now.

How are we connected to Kickstarter?

We've created our own independent project on Kickstarter! If you would like to help fund the game you can donate, donating is optional and another way of supporting the game, but not necessary. It doesn't matter how small or large the sum is, every support we get is greatly appreciated.
